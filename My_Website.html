What is Html?

HTML stands for HyperText Markup Language and is the standard language used to create and structure web pages, defining the content, layout, and elements like headings, paragraphs, images, and links that are displayed in a web browser. It uses a system of "tags" enclosed in angle brackets to mark up content and organize it into semantic elements, which tells the browser how to render the content on the screen.

What HTML does:

Structures content:

HTML provides the basic building blocks for a webpage, allowing you to add headings, paragraphs, lists, tables, images, and more.

Adds semantics:

HTML elements give meaning to content, such as identifying a particular piece of text as a paragraph (p) or a heading H1.

Integral University

Integral University: An Overview Integral University is a prominent private university located in Lucknow, the capital city of Uttar Pradesh, India. Established in 1998, the university has grown to become one of the leading educational institutions in the region. It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and offers a wide array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including engineering, management, law, science, pharmacy, and healthcare. With a vision to provide comprehensive education, Integral University aims to cultivate skilled professionals who can contribute to the development of society. Academic Programs Integral University offers diverse academic programs tailored to meet the needs of various fields. The university’s curriculum focuses on both theoretical knowledge and practical learning, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the challenges of the real world. Engineering and Technology: Integral University is particularly well-known for its engineering programs. The university offers undergraduate programs (B.Tech) in several branches, including Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Electronics Engineering. Additionally, postgraduate programs (M.Tech) in specialized areas like Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and VLSI Design are also available. The engineering curriculum emphasizes innovation, research, and practical exposure through labs, projects, and internships. Management: Integral University offers a comprehensive MBA program with specializations in fields like Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, and International Business. The university’s management program is designed to equip students with leadership skills, strategic thinking, and business acumen. The curriculum integrates case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts to ensure a holistic understanding of business practices. Science: The university offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various science disciplines such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. Research-oriented programs provide opportunities for students to explore emerging fields like Biotechnology and Environmental Science, contributing to academic advancements and sustainable development. Law: Integral University has a law school offering undergraduate programs like BA LLB and LLB, along with postgraduate programs such as LLM. The law curriculum is structured to provide a thorough understanding of the Indian legal system, international law, and human rights, with practical training through moot courts and internships. Pharmacy: Integral University offers both undergraduate and postgraduate programs in pharmacy, such as B.Pharm and M.Pharm. The university's pharmacy department focuses on pharmaceutical sciences, drug development, clinical research, and healthcare management. Medical and Health Sciences: The university has a School of Health Sciences that offers programs in areas like Nursing, Physiotherapy, and Allied Health Sciences. These programs are designed to meet the 19 growing demand for healthcare professionals in the country. The curriculum is geared toward providing hands-on experience and clinical exposure. Commerce and Arts: Integral University also offers programs in Commerce, Economics, and Business Administration. These programs aim to prepare students for careers in finance, business, and economics, with a strong focus on practical skills and research methodologies. Education: The university offers B.Ed. and M.Ed. programs that focus on producing qualified and skilled educators. These programs are designed to equip future teachers with the latest pedagogical techniques and educational theories. Research and Innovation Integral University places a significant emphasis on research and innovation, encouraging students and faculty to explore new ideas and contribute to various fields of knowledge. The university has established various research centers and is involved in projects related to technology, health, environmental sustainability, and more. Students are encouraged to participate in research activities, including publishing papers, presenting at conferences, and collaborating with industry partners for practical exposure.
